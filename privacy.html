Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 17, 2025

At THE Randoms, we respect your privacy. This page explains what information we do — and do not — collect when you use our site.

Information We Collect

THE Randoms does not require personal information to use the site. We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information.

Some basic, non-identifying information may be automatically collected, such as browser type, device type, and general usage statistics. This helps us improve the site and ensure it works correctly across devices.

Cookies

We may use simple cookies or local storage to remember your settings or game progress. These are stored on your device and never shared with others.

Third-Party Services

THE Randoms does not sell, trade, or share your personal data. If we link to third-party services or content, those sites may have their own privacy practices, which we are not responsible for.

Your Control

You can clear cookies or local storage at any time through your browser settings. Doing so may reset preferences or progress on the site.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the site grows. If changes are made, the date above will be updated to reflect the latest version.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out through the contact information on THE Randoms website.
